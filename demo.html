Welcome to our Website

This is the main content area that users with disabilities might have difficulty reaching because there's no way to skip past the navigation blocks.

For keyboard users, they must tab through all navigation items before reaching this content.

This violates WCAG 2.4.1 which requires a mechanism to bypass blocks of content that are repeated on multiple pages.
